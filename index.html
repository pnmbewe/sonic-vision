About

The Job Application app was founded in 2022AD out of the frustrations experienced by the founder when applying for jobs, analogue and digital. In simple terms, the Job Application app solves the founder’s problem. Seeing multitudes put in the strenuous effort, limited time and last penny applying for jobs they would not get inspires the Job Application app team to continually innovate using digital means.

Features

Job Alerts

 Users should be able to search for job openings and apply for positions directly through the platform.

Personalized job recommendations

The platform provides personalized job recommendations based on a user's skills, experience, and career goals.

Multilingual support

The platform should supports multiple languages, allowing users from different provinces and regions to access and use the platform.

Making Job Search Easy.

Mission

We aim to become the go-to job application App at the center of the job recruitment ecosystem and bring the right opportunities to the right individuals, Making job applications easier, more purposeful, trusted, and matched.
